# Accounting: V2 Enhancement Sources

## Industry/Vendor
- **KPMG Clara** - AI-powered audit platform for full-population transaction analysis
- **Deloitte Omnia** - ML-based anomaly detection in journal entries and financial records
- **EY Canvas** - AI-integrated audit workflow platform
- **PwC Halo / GL.ai** - Analytical environment for ledger-level AI analysis; GL.ai for audit anomaly detection (built with H2O.ai, won Audit Innovation of the Year)
- **BlackLine** - Automated account reconciliation platform
- **MindBridge** - AI transaction risk scoring and anomaly detection
- **Thomson Reuters ONESOURCE** - NLP-driven tax research and compliance
- **Wolters Kluwer CCH Axcess** - AI-assisted tax research platform
- **Workiva** - AI cross-referencing for financial statements and filings
- **Kofax** - OCR and ML for invoice and document data extraction
- **ABBYY** - Intelligent document processing for accounting workflows
- **Blue J** - AI-powered tax research platform (named in AICPA 2025 AI report)
- **Caseware AiDA** - Digital assistant for audit workflows
- **Karbon** - Agentic AI features for accounting practice management
