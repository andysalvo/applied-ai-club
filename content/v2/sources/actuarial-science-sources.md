# Actuarial Science: V2 Enhancement Sources

## Industry/Vendor
- **Akur8** - Transparent ML-driven insurance pricing platform
- **Earnix** - Dynamic pricing for auto, home, and commercial insurance lines
- **Milliman (Arius)** - AI-augmented reserving and loss estimation platform
- **WTW (Willis Towers Watson)** - AI reserving tools and multi-method model comparison
- **Shift Technology** - AI fraud detection for insurance claims
- **Lemonade** - AI-driven claims processing for straightforward cases
- **Verisk (AIR platform)** - Catastrophe modeling with ML components
- **Moody's RMS** - Catastrophe risk modeling and analytics
- **CoreLogic** - Property-level catastrophe exposure data and modeling

  - "Predictive Analytics and Machine Learning -- Practical Applications" (2023): https://www.soa.org/resources/research-reports/2023/predictive-analytics-and-machine-learning/

  - "Machine Learning in Insurance" (Winter 2022 E-Forum): https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2022-03/01_Winter-Eforum-2022-ML_in_Insurance.pdf
- **American Academy of Actuaries** - Professional responsibility standards for AI-assisted work
