AI by Smeal Major

Interactive learning modules that help Smeal students understand how AI is discussed and applied in the context of their own major. Built by students, for students.

All 10 Smeal Majors

Accounting Actuarial Science Corporate Innovation Finance Management MIS Marketing Real Estate Risk Management Supply Chain

How We Built It

1

Students wrote the first draft

Club members selected a Smeal major, studied the curriculum at undergrad.smeal.psu.edu, and used a generative AI writing assistant to draft content following shared templates and tone rules. The content is descriptive, not instructional.

2

Research and enhancement

Each draft was enhanced with real-world examples, named tools, and source citations from industry reports, professional associations, and academic publications. Over 120 unique sources across all 10 majors.

3

Designed and built into interactive modules

The content was transformed into polished, interactive HTML modules with section navigation, key points, and original imagery. Each module covers four areas: where AI appears, what it helps with, where it falls short, and what to keep in mind.

4

Deployed on the Student AI Hub

The finished modules are embedded in the Student AI Hub, a Wix-hosted site under the Penn State Smeal College of Business. Every major has its own page with a consistent, accessible interface.

What each module covers

  • Where AI appears in the field and why it comes up in coursework and industry practice
  • What AI is expected to help with, from data processing to scenario analysis to workflow efficiency
  • Where AI falls short, including limits around judgment, bias, explainability, and regime change
  • Key considerations for students and faculty, including governance, accountability, and professional readiness
