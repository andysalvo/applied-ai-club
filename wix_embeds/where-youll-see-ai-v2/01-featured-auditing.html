Featured Domain

Auditing

AI shows up first in audit planning and transaction testing when manual sampling stops being enough.

In accounting programs, this appears in discussions about how reviewers decide where to look closely when time is limited. In practice, firms use AI-supported review to scan broader transaction sets and detect irregular patterns earlier.

This does not replace auditor judgment. It supports prioritization: AI can widen coverage and surface exceptions, but humans still assess materiality, context, and final conclusions.

Student lens: expect interview questions about how you would validate flagged items instead of trusting model output by default.
