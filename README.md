This repository is meant to keep track of progress on the Student AI Hub. 

The Student AI Hub is fully operational hub for Penn State Smeal College of Business Students to get up to date information and guidance regarding Artificial Intelligence use at Penn State. 

The Penn State Smeal College of Business prepares students to make informed decisions and act with integrity. It emphasizes analytical thinking, ethical judgment, and understanding real-world business problems.
Students entering the workforce will be expected not only to use AI tools, but to evaluate their outputs, understand their limitations, and operate within clear ethical and professional standards.
The Hub exists to support that preparation.

Repository structure:

- `canonical/` contains the current canonical Smeal major content files.
- `supporting/` contains supporting materials such as embeddings, Wix embed assets, and workflow notes.
- `archive/` contains non-canonical duplicates and older artifacts kept for reference.
