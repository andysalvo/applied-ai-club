Accounting Workflow Detail

How AI creates leverage across the close, review, and audit cycle

  • Models scan high-volume ledgers to surface outliers by amount, timing, vendor behavior, and posting pattern before month-end close.
  • Document extraction and coding suggestions accelerate invoice-to-entry flow, while confidence scores direct human review to higher-risk records.
  • AI-assisted reconciliations match subledger and GL entries faster, then prioritize unresolved breaks with context for follow-up evidence.
  • Continuous monitoring signals strengthen audit readiness by flagging unusual controls activity and missing support before testing windows.
