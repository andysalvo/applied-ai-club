Context for Students

Where AI appears is shaped by the gap between classroom problem sets and firm-scale accounting workflows.

In Class

Students often work with smaller, cleaner datasets in auditing, reporting, tax, and controls coursework.

In Practice

Organizations review continuous streams across many accounts and entities, so AI supports scale, consistency, and prioritization.

Three Practical Takeaways

  1. Expect AI to appear where transaction volume is highest.
  2. Use model output as a review signal, not as a final conclusion.
  3. Documentation and explainability matter as much as speed.

This section is intentionally descriptive, not instructional. The goal is to help students recognize where AI fits in accounting workflows while keeping professional judgment and accountability central.
